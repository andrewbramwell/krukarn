FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Do I need prior experience?

    No prior experience needed. Our courses suit both beginners and experienced therapists looking to expand their services.

  • 2. What qualifications do I need to open a massage business in the UK?

    VTCT Thai Massage Level 3 certification is required for UK council licensing. Requirements vary by country.

  • 3. Do I need accommodation for training?

    The VTCT course is 2 days, while other courses are 1 day. A Premier Inn is nearby, or accommodation at Kru Karn's home may be available.

  • 4. Can you provide training at my business location?

    Yes, for groups of 4 or more students. Contact us to arrange on-site training.

  • 5. Are the qualifications internationally recognised?

    VTCT certification is accepted on cruise ships and in several countries. Check specific requirements for your destination.

  • 6. Do you use mannequins for practice?

    All training is conducted with real people to ensure authentic learning experience.
